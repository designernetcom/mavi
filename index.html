About Mavi

Leading the Way in Industrial Safety and Energy Savings.

At Mavi Energy, innovation powers everything we do. With over three decades of expertise inherited from our sister company, S.S. Enterprises, and the legacy of our trusted brands, Autoweld/Weldon, we have revolutionized energy-saving solutions for welding machines.

Our flagship product, the Voltage Reducing Device (VRD) cum Energy Saver, sets a benchmark in safety and efficiency for both AC and DC welding machines. But we don’t stop there. Our expanding portfolio includes cyclic timers, digital timer panels, Change Over Panels, motion sensors, and a range of advanced energy-saving devices designed to optimize industrial processes. Additionally, our expertise extends to cutting-edge air compressors and vacuum pumps, offering comprehensive solutions for diverse operational needs.

For over 27 years, Mavi Energy has been at the forefront of designing and executing advanced Medical Gas Pipeline Systems (MGPS) for hospitals across India. With our roots in Global Energy Management Services, we have delivered over 300+ projects, offering end-to-end solutions that redefine reliability and safety in healthcare infrastructure.

Our expertise encompasses consultation, design, and execution of MGPS projects, tailored to the specific needs of healthcare facilities. Trusted by renowned institutions like Ruby Hall Clinic Pune and D.Y. Patil Institute Pune, we are proud to set benchmarks in quality and innovation for the medical industry.
